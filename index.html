Core features

IronScape combines structured workout logging with a dark RPG-inspired presentation, turning training consistency into visible progression.

⬆

Skill-based progression

Train muscle groups and activities to earn XP and build a profile that grows over time like a character progression system.

⌁

Fatigue-aware training

Monitor training load and recovery so hard sessions feel rewarding without ignoring the realities of fatigue management.

◫

Workout builder

Create, save, and reuse custom sessions while tracking sets, reps, weight, and effort with a clean mobile workflow.

◔

Nutrition tracking

Log meals, calories, and macros so your training and nutrition systems live together in one app experience.

▣

Dark RPG aesthetic

Designed to feel more like progression and less like bookkeeping, with a visual identity that fits the IronScape concept.

↗

Progress dashboard

See how skill levels, XP, and consistency evolve over time so every session contributes to a larger arc of progress.
