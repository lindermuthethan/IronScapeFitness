IronScape Fitness Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

IronScape Fitness (“IronScape”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the IronScape Fitness mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

IronScape may collect the following types of information when you use the app:

  • Workout logs and training activity
  • Nutrition and meal tracking data
  • Progress metrics such as skill levels or XP
  • Basic device information used for app performance

IronScape does not collect sensitive personal information such as government IDs, financial account numbers, or unrelated personal data.

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used only to operate and improve the IronScape application, including:

  • Providing workout tracking features
  • Saving your progress and training data
  • Improving app performance and stability
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues

Data Storage

Most data entered into IronScape is stored locally on your device. This allows the application to function even without an internet connection and helps limit unnecessary data sharing.

Third-Party Services

IronScape may use third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, or app distribution platforms such as the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. These services may collect limited technical information required to operate their platforms.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect your information and maintain application security. However, no software or internet transmission is completely secure.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Delete your data by removing the application from your device
  • Contact support to request additional information

Children's Privacy

IronScape is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
