Common Questions

How does XP work?

IronScape converts your workouts into experience points (XP). Different exercises contribute to different muscle skill categories such as chest, back, legs, cardio, and more.

Does IronScape require an internet connection?

No. Most functionality works offline because workout and nutrition data are stored locally on your device.

Will my progress be saved if I close the app?

Yes. All logged workouts, meals, and XP progression are saved locally so you can resume anytime.

How do I report a bug?

Send an email to support@ironscapefitness.com describing:

  • What happened
  • What you expected to happen
  • Your device type (iPhone model if possible)
  • The app version

Feature Requests

IronScape is actively being improved. If you have ideas for new features, progression mechanics, or training tools you'd like to see in the app, feel free to send them to our support email.

Troubleshooting

  • Ensure the app is updated to the latest version.
  • Restart the app if a screen appears stuck.
  • Restart your device if issues persist.
  • If problems continue, contact support.
